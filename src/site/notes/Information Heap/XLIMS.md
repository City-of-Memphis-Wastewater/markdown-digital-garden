Here is the process for downloading results from XLIMS, by sample location, to share with CDM smith

1. Go to **Reporting**
https://xlims.memphistn.gov/lmEntry.aspx?LandingScreen=ReportingDLG.aspx%3FInterfaceID%3D264
2. Go to **Favorites**
3. Go to **Results by Sample Loc(2)**
4. For Sample Location, Click the spreadsheet button (not the dropdown button)
	1. Navigate pages using the four blue buttons in the upper right hand corner, to go to the last two pages
	2. Get data for (7) different aspects:
		- Operations (T.E. Maxson Operations Data)
		- Primary Influent (PRII)
		- Primary effluent (PRIE)
		- trickling filter influent (ABFI)
		- trickling filter effluent (ABFE)
		- plant influent  (INF)
		- plant effluent (FE)
