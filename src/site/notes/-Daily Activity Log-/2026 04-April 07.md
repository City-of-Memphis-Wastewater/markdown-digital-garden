## Grit tank auto sampler
Autosampler off of Grit tank, model 6712 Teladyne ISCO. And then send samples off for analysis. It holds 20 liters, 24 hour composite. Flow-based: More flow, more samples. On a normal day, 6 liters expect. Sample is taken to [[Vendors/Waypoint Analytic\|Waypoint Analytic]]. Flow data sent from Ovation system to the sampler, so that it can do its sample rate calculation. An accessory converts the 4-20 signal to number  pulses
- COD
- BOD
- TSS
- Temp
